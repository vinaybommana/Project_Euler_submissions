# Problem 1 (Multiples of 3 and 5)

\begin{equation}
sum = 3 * \frac{n (n + 1)}{2} + 5 * \frac{n (n + 1)}{2} - 15 * \frac{n (n + 1)} {2}
\end{equation}

* n is the number one less than given N
* 15 is subtracted because we are adding numbers which are multiples of 3 and 5 both.
